Asenso Global Gardens and View Deck

Asenso Global Gardens and View Deck is an ecotourism destination located near the Hoyohoy Adventure Park. It is situated almost 1,000 feet above sea level and covers 191 hectares of land. The garden's landscape is filled with lush vegetation and provides a clear view of Tangub, Ozamiz, and the nearby municipalities.

Construction is not yet finished; however, it is open to the public.

Kafehan

Kafehan is a renowned spot in Tangub that provides a cozy environment for locals and tourists alike. It blends delicious treats with the natural beauty of the surrounding landscape.

Hoyohoy Highland Chapel

Hoyohoy Highland Stone Chapel Adventure Park is a nature park located on the outskirts of the city. It provides a good view of Tangub and Panguil Bay. The park features a stone chapel, an organic herbal garden, and the longest zipline in Asia.

Located at the foot of Mt. Malindang, it offers a majestic view of the sunrise and Panguil Bay.
